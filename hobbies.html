My Hobbies

Hey there! Let's talk about the things I love to do!

Cooking

I really enjoy making good food. I have to cook a lot because my family and I are intolerant to so many foods, but I still find it satisfying.

What I love about cooking

  • Being up and moving around my kitchen
  • Baking breads can be so therapeutic
  • Finding a new recipe that my son loves

Graphic Design

Here I really am a hobbiest, but I enjoy exercising the creative part of my brain.

What I enjoy creating

  • T-shirt designs! My favorite project so far has been my day of the week shirts I designed for my son. I mainly use a website called Kittl to make my designs.
  • Greeting Cards! I enjoy making handmade greeting cards for my friends and relatives. I either purchase or create my own designs and then cut them out on my Silhouette Machine.
  • Websites! While I'm still very much a novice here, I so enjoy the process of turning code into something beautiful.

Photography

While my main subject has grown beyond being willing, I still enjoy taking pictures. I took pictures for my son's school for many years, and I loved capturing the faces and special moments of the students.
